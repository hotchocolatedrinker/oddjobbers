About Us

Here at Odd Jobbers, we’re a husband-and-wife team dedicated to bringing high-quality craftsmanship and green-fingered care to homes across Herne Bay. With years of experience in our respective trades, we decided to combine our skills and launch a business that offers both expert carpentry and professional gardening services.


Stuart is a skilled carpenter with a passion for precision, whether it's custom furniture, decking, fencing, or repairs. Joanna has spent years cultivating gardens, from simple maintenance to full landscaping projects, ensuring every outdoor space thrives.


We take pride in our work, offering a personal, friendly service with attention to detail and a commitment to quality. Whether you're looking to refresh your garden, build something bespoke, or tackle those odd jobs around the house, we’re here to help.
